==========

Agile Delivery Managers require a high level of emotional intelligence to effectively manage teams and successfully deliver projects in an agile environment. Emotional intelligence is a crucial competency for Agile Delivery Managers as it helps them to communicate, collaborate, and build trust with team members and stakeholders, handle conflict and manage the stress, and lead the team through change.

The skills required for a competent level of emotional intelligence include:

1. Self-awareness: Agile Delivery Managers should be able to recognize their own emotions and understand how they impact their behavior, decisions, and relationships with others. Self-awareness helps them navigate difficult situations, recognize their strengths and weaknesses, and seek support or feedback when needed.

2. Self-regulation: Agile Delivery Managers should be able to manage their emotions in a way that aligns with the needs of the team and organization, and recognize when it is appropriate to express emotions and when it is necessary to regulate them. They should also be able to adapt quickly to changes and handle stress and pressure effectively.

3. Motivation: Agile Delivery Managers should be motivated to achieve the goals of the team and organization and inspire motivation in others. They should set high expectations for themselves and the team, be optimistic and positive in their attitude, and celebrate successes along the way.

4. Empathy: Agile Delivery Managers should be able to understand and respond to the emotions of the team and stakeholders, and recognize the needs and perspectives of others. This can help them to build trust, foster collaboration, and improve communication.

5. Social skills: Agile Delivery Managers should be skilled in communication, teamwork, and relationship-building. This includes active listening, effective feedback, conflict resolution, and coaching and mentoring. They should also be able to facilitate meetings, build coalitions, and promote open and honest communication.

To develop these skills, Agile Delivery Managers can practice various techniques such as meditation, journaling, self-reflection, and seeking feedback from their team and stakeholders. They can also attend training programs or coaching sessions to develop these skills further.

In conclusion, emotional intelligence is a key competency for Agile Delivery Managers, and a competent level of emotional intelligence requires skills in self-awareness, self-regulation, motivation, empathy, and social skills. By developing these skills, Agile Delivery Managers can effectively manage teams and deliver projects in an agile environment.
