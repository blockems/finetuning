Facilitation, in an Agile context, refers to the ability to help teams work together, brainstorm ideas, and solve problems effectively. Expert 4: Another critical competency required for facilitation in Agile project management is emotional intelligence. An Agile project manager must be aware of their emotional state and those of team members. They must be able to empathize with their issues and manage conflicts positively.
