==========

Agile delivery managers need to have a strong understanding of budget management to effectively manage their projects. In order to be considered competent in this competency, they require a combination of skills, practices, and areas of knowledge.

Skills:

1. Financial analysis and forecasting: Agile delivery managers should be able to analyze financial data and recognize trends to make informed predictions about future expenses and resource needs.

2. Negotiation: They must be capable negotiators, able to secure necessary resources and budget approvals from stakeholders.

3. Risk management: They should be able to identify and mitigate financial risks that may impact project budget.

Practices:

1. Budget forecasting: Agile delivery managers should begin each project by creating a projected budget for the entire project.

2. Resource allocation: They should ensure that resources are allocated appropriately throughout the project.

3. Cost tracking and control: Agile delivery managers should monitor costs throughout the project lifecycle and take action to control expenses when necessary.

Areas of knowledge:

1. Financial management: Understanding basic financial principles and practices is crucial for managing project budget.

2. Agile methodologies: Familiarity with agile methodologies and principles is essential for effectively managing budget in an agile environment.

3. Project management: Knowledge of project management processes, tools and techniques enable Agile delivery managers to manage budget with greater efficiency.

Reference:

Project Management Institute. (2017). Agile Practice Guide. 1st ed. Project Management Institute.
