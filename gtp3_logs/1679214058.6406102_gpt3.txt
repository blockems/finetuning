==========

Agile Delivery Managers are expected to possess a strong set of leadership competencies that enable them to cultivate a culture of agility within an organization. One of the essential competencies required to achieve this is Lean-Agile Leadership. This competency refers to the ability to lead and manage the agile transformation in an organization.

To be at the level of Emergent for the competency Lean-Agile Leadership, an Agile Delivery Manager must possess the following skills, practices, and Areas of knowledge:

Skills:
- Transformational leadership: The Agile Delivery Manager should have the ability to inspire change and motivate people to adopt an agile mindset.
- Facilitation: The Agile Delivery Manager should have the ability to facilitate meetings and ceremonies to promote collaboration and co-creation.
- Coaching and Mentoring: The Agile Delivery Manager should have the ability to coach and mentor individuals and teams to reinforce agile practices.
- Systems thinking: The Agile Delivery Manager should have the ability to see the big picture and understand how various parts of the organization interact with each other.
- Emotional intelligence: The Agile Delivery Manager should have the ability to understand and manage their emotions, as well as those of others.
 
Practices:
- Continuous improvement: The Agile Delivery Manager should promote a culture of continuous improvement by encouraging feedback loops, experimentation, and learning.
- Value-driven delivery: The Agile Delivery Manager should ensure that the team delivers value to the customer through close collaboration and frequent delivery of working software.
- Scrum framework: The Agile Delivery Manager should have a thorough understanding of the Scrum framework and be able to coach and mentor teams on how to apply it effectively.
- Lean thinking: The Agile Delivery Manager should understand and apply Lean principles to eliminate waste, optimize flow, and deliver value.
 
Areas of knowledge:
- Agile frameworks and methodologies: The Agile Delivery Manager should have a broad understanding of Agile frameworks and methodologies, including Scrum, Kanban, XP, and SAFe.
- Agile engineering practices: The Agile Delivery Manager should understand Agile engineering practices such as Test-Driven Development (TDD), Continuous Integration (CI), and Continuous Deployment (CD).
- Lean principles: The Agile Delivery Manager should understand and apply Lean principles such as Value Stream Mapping, Visual Management, and Pull systems.
- Change management: The Agile Delivery Manager should have a deep understanding of change management principles and practices to facilitate the agile transformation.
