Stakeholders include anyone who is impacted by the change initiative, whether positively or negatively. Agile coaches must be able to engage with stakeholders effectively, build trust, and manage their expectations throughout the change process. Building trust is crucial in change initiatives, and Agile coaches must establish trust with all stakeholders. They must be able to understand the needs of team members and leaders, while also balancing these needs against the goals of the change initiative.
